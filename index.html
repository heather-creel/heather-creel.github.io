About Me

From Maryland to Tennessee: I'm a Maryland native who moved to Florida where I lived for 10 years, had both my kids, and have been calling Tennessee home since 2011. I've been organizing things my entire life - as a kid, I was the one planning elaborate backyard talent shows and convincing my dad to make homework copies so we could "play school." My siblings had a different word for it - bossy.

The Unconventional Path: I took the road less traveled - skipping college right after high school, working as a waitress, and earning my degree while juggling work and raising two kids. My corporate break came when a fellow waitress mentioned her company had an opening.

Finding My Groove: They took a chance on me, and I worked my way up, refining processes and really finding my groove in operations. It wasn't until I moved to Tennessee and joined ADP that I got my first real exposure to new technologies like Salesforce.

Life Today: Now I'm living in Tennessee with my husband (married since 2004), our two teenagers, and our two dogs - a senior and a puppy. When I'm not optimizing business systems, you'll find me enjoying silly humor, sleeping in, planning travel adventures, tending to my garden, or working on puzzles.

I'm passionate about leveraging technology to solve real business problems and helping others navigate their career journeys. I embrace cutting-edge tools including AI to deliver results efficiently - even this website was created through AI collaboration. Currently seeking my next challenge as a VP of Business Systems where I can continue transforming how organizations work.
